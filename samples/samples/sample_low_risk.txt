STANDARD BUSINESS AGREEMENT

This agreement is entered between Company A and Company B.

1. Termination  
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

2. Liability  
Liability is capped to the total contract value.

3. Dispute Resolution  
Both parties agree to resolve disputes amicably before legal action.

4. Intellectual Property  
Each party retains ownership of their respective intellectual property.

5. Renewal  
Renewal shall be mutually agreed in writing.

6. Jurisdiction  
This agreement shall be governed by Indian law.

7. Confidentiality  
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality.

This agreement represents a balanced contractual relationship.
