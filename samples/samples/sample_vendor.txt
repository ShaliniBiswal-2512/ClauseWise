VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on [Date], by and between RetailCorp Pvt. Ltd., having its registered office at [Company Address] (“Company”), and [Vendor Name], having its principal place of business at [Vendor Address] (“Vendor”).

Both parties agree as follows:

1. Scope of Services  
Vendor agrees to provide goods and/or services as mutually agreed in writing from time to time in accordance with Company standards.

2. Term & Termination  
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain valid for one (1) year unless terminated earlier.  
Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days written notice.  
The Company may terminate immediately in case of material breach or misconduct.

3. Payment Terms  
Company shall make payments within thirty (30) days of receipt of a valid invoice.  
All prices shall be inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.

4. Penalties & Breach  
In case of failure to meet agreed deliverables, Vendor may be subject to penalties up to INR 40,000 depending on the severity of the breach.

5. Liability  
Vendor shall be liable for direct damages caused due to negligence or breach, subject to a maximum of the total contract value.

6. Indemnification  
Vendor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any third-party claims arising out of Vendor’s services, negligence, or violation of laws.

7. Intellectual Property  
All work products, documents, or materials created under this Agreement shall become the exclusive property of the Company unless otherwise agreed in writing.

8. Confidentiality  
Vendor shall keep all Company information confidential during and after the term of this Agreement.

9. Compliance With Laws  
Vendor agrees to comply with all applicable Indian laws, regulations, and tax requirements.

10. Dispute Resolution  
Any disputes shall first be resolved amicably. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11. Governing Law & Jurisdiction  
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Courts at Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
