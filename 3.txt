VENDOR CONTRACT

This Vendor Agreement is entered between Retail Corp and Vendor.

1. Termination
Retail Corp reserves the right to terminate this agreement at any time without notice.

2. Penalty
Vendor shall pay a penalty of INR 40,000 for any breach of contract.

3. Liability
Vendor agrees to unlimited liability for loss or damage caused.

4. Indemnity
Vendor shall indemnify Retail Corp for all legal expenses and claims.

5. Jurisdiction
Mumbai courts shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

6. Arbitration
All disputes shall be resolved by arbitration.

7. Intellectual Property
Any materials created shall become property of Retail Corp.

By executing this contract, Vendor accepts all obligations.
