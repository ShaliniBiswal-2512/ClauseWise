EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd (“Employer”) and the Employee.

1. Termination
The Employer may terminate this agreement at any time without notice. The Employee shall pay a penalty of INR 25,000 if resignation occurs within 12 months.

2. Liability
The Employee accepts unlimited liability for any losses caused directly or indirectly.

3. Indemnity
The Employee agrees to indemnify the Employer against all claims, damages, or expenses arising from breach of this agreement.

4. Non-Compete
The Employee shall not engage in any competing business for a period of two years after termination.

5. Intellectual Property
All intellectual property created during employment shall belong exclusively to the Employer.

6. Jurisdiction
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of Bangalore jurisdiction.

7. Arbitration
Any dispute arising shall be resolved by mandatory arbitration.

By signing below, both parties agree to all terms stated herein.
