STANDARD BUSINESS AGREEMENT

This agreement is between Company A and Company B.

1. Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

2. Liability
Liability shall be capped to the value of the contract.

3. Dispute Resolution
Parties agree to resolve disputes amicably.

4. Intellectual Property
Each party retains ownership of their respective IP.

5. Jurisdiction
This agreement shall be governed by Indian law.

6. Renewal
Renewal shall be mutually agreed.

This agreement represents a balanced contractual relationship.
